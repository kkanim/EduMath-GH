Our Ghanaian Education Story

Empowering students through WAEC-aligned maths education

Our Mission

To transform maths education in Ghana through technology and culturally relevant teaching methods.

Founded in Accra in 2018, EduMath GH was created by Ghanaian educators who recognized the need for high-quality, locally relevant maths resources aligned with WAEC standards.

We believe every Ghanaian student deserves access to excellent maths instruction, regardless of their school's location or resources.

Our Vision

To become Ghana's most trusted maths education partner, helping schools nationwide achieve 95%+ pass rates in WAEC exams.

Our Values

Quality, Relevance, Accessibility, and Excellence in everything we do for Ghanaian education.

Our Reach

Currently serving schools in all 16 regions of Ghana, with special programs for rural communities.

Our Impact

Over 5,000 students supported with average score improvements of 25% in national exams.

Our Story

Kwame Osei

Founder & CEO

"As a former maths teacher in Kumasi, I saw firsthand how students struggled with concepts that weren't taught in Ghanaian context. The 'If 5 oranges cost $1...' problems in foreign textbooks didn't resonate with our students."

After completing my Masters in Education Technology, I gathered a team of Ghanaian curriculum experts and developers to create EduMath GH in 2018. We started with just 3 schools in Ashanti Region, and through word-of-mouth from teachers seeing real results, we've grown to serve schools nationwide.
